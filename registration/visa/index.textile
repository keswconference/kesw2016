p<>. A valid passport is required to visit the Czech Republic.

p<>. An up-to-date list of countries qualified for visa-less visit to the Czech Republic can be found at the website of the "Czech Ministry of Foreign Affairs":http://www.mzv.cz/jnp/en/information_for_aliens/general_visa_information/. You can also check with the Embassy or Consulate in your country about regulations for your nationality.

h3. No Visa Required

Citizens of most EU countries, US, Israel, and some other countries do not need a visa for short-term visits.

h3. Visa Required

The company handling conference registration ("Action M Agency":http://www.action-m.com/) will be also providing visa support.
